Mountain Gorilla

Status: Endangered

Population: more than 1,000

they live in east-central Africa in national parks in Uganda, Rwanda and Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) and one in Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, Uganda, which connects to Sarambwe Nature Reserve

Snow Leopard

Status: Vulnerable

Population: 4,000 - 6,500

They live across 12 countries in central and south Asia, from southern Russia down to the Tibetan plateau, including Mongolia, China, Afghanistan, Pakistan, India and Nepal and are usally in mountainus regions
